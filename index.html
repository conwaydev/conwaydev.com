Insights

Insights is Sprout Social's WordPress blog where content creators are able to create thought leadership pieces on industry best practices in social media. Before I started at Sprout Social, Insights had gone through 2 different themes and had amassed a large amount of technical debt along the way. I was able to make the case for a complete retheming, from the ground up in order to increase performance as well as making it easier for new developers to get up to speed with the codebase.
